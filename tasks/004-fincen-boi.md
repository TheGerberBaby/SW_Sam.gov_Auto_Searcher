## Why this matters

The Corporate Transparency Act requires most U.S. business entities to
report beneficial ownership to FinCEN. Filing is free.

> ⚠️ The BOI reporting landscape has been litigated and re-litigated
> through 2024–2026. **Re-verify the current deadline and applicability
> at filing time** — Treasury rules and federal court injunctions have
> changed the effective date multiple times.
