## Why this matters

- **Mandatory** since 2024-12-22 for SDVOSB set-asides to count toward
  the federal goal. Self-certification no longer counts.
- The SDVOSB federal goal was raised from **3% to 5%** by FY2024 NDAA
  Sec. 863 (PL 118-31, enacted 2023-12-22). SDVOSBs received **$31.9 B
  (5%)** of federal contracting dollars in FY2024 per SBA.
- The function moved from VA CVE to SBA on 2023-01-01.
- SBA cleared its backlog in Nov 2025 and reports a **12-day average
  processing time** (down from 81 days at end of 2024).
- Free to file.

## Requirements (per 13 CFR Part 128)

- 51%+ unconditional ownership and control by a service-disabled
  veteran (any rating, even 0% — qualifying disability is the gate).
- Small under the primary NAICS (verify NAICS 541512 size standard
  at the moment of filing against [13 CFR 121.201](https://www.sba.gov/document/support--table-size-standards)).
- Active SAM.gov registration (see [002](002-sam-gov-registration.md)).
- DD-214.
- VA disability letter.

## Subtasks

- [ ] Locate DD-214
- [ ] Locate or request current VA disability letter
- [ ] Confirm 51%+ ownership/control documented (operating agreement)
- [ ] Verify NAICS 541512 size standard at current filing date
- [ ] Confirm SAM.gov registration is `Active`
- [ ] Create / log into VetCert account at <https://veterans.certify.sba.gov>
- [ ] Submit application
- [ ] Set calendar reminder for ~14 days post-submission to follow up

## Outcomes to record when done

- Certification effective date
- Certification number
- Renewal/recertification date (typically 3 years)
