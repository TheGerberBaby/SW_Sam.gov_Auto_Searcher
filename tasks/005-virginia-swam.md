## Why this matters

- Unlocks Virginia state set-aside / preference for Stormwind under
  the Department of Small Business and Supplier Diversity (DSBSD)
  Small, Women-owned, and Minority-owned (SWaM) program.
- Service-disabled designation at the state level requires a
  separate Virginia Department of Veterans Services (DVS) letter.
- No fee.
- Independent of federal SDVOSB via [003](003-sdvosb-vetcert.md) —
  Virginia maintains its own register.

## Subtasks

- [ ] Request Virginia DVS service-disabled veteran certification
      letter
- [ ] Gather entity documents (formation, EIN, ownership)
- [ ] Create / log into DSBSD vendor account at
      <https://www.sbsd.virginia.gov/>
- [ ] Submit SWaM application
- [ ] Submit SDVOSB designation request alongside SWaM
- [ ] Track approval

Pair with [006-eva-registration.md](006-eva-registration.md) so that
once SWaM clears, Virginia procurement vendors can identify Stormwind
in eVA.
