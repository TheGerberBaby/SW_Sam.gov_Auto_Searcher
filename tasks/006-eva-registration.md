## Why this matters

eVA is Virginia's procurement portal. Most state and many local
Virginia agencies post and receive quotes through eVA. Registration
is the access door for SWaM-flagged opportunities (see
[005](005-virginia-swam.md)).

## Subtasks

- [ ] Create eVA vendor account at <https://eva.virginia.gov/>
- [ ] Provide entity / EIN / SAM UEI
- [ ] Set NAICS / NIGP commodity codes (include 541512 and adjacent
      IT codes; map to eVA's NIGP coding)
- [ ] Configure notification subscriptions for relevant NIGP codes /
      Virginia agencies
- [ ] Verify SWaM linkage once SWaM is approved
