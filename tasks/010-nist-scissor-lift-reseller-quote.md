## Why this is a plausible home-office deal

NIST wants one new commercial electric scissor lift. Stormwind can coordinate
the transaction from a home office if an authorized dealer supports the exact
configuration, direct delivery, demonstration, training, manuals, warranty,
and reseller documentation.

This is not an approved bid yet. Do not submit until the supplier quote,
authorization, cash-flow plan, and nonmanufacturer-rule check are complete.

## Required configuration

- One new electric scissor lift with 110VAC charger
- Indoor use on flat, level, hard surfaces
- Minimum 25-foot platform height
- 500-pound platform capacity at maximum height
- Folding rails
- Zero turning radius
- Minimum 36-inch deck extension
- Dual flashing warning lights
- Tilt alarm
- Three-year warranty
- Delivery, demonstration, and initial training for four operators by an
  authorized manufacturer's representative
- Operator, parts, and service manuals
- Delivery within 120 calendar days after award to NIST Gaithersburg

Amendment `0004` clarifies that the platform must carry 500 pounds; the
extension deck itself does not need a 500-pound capacity. New equipment only.

## Supplier RFQ

Send this request to authorized equipment dealers:

> Please quote one new electric scissor lift for direct delivery to NIST,
> Gaithersburg, MD within 120 calendar days after award. Include freight,
> authorized-manufacturer-representative demonstration and training for four
> operators, operator/parts/service manuals, and a three-year warranty.
> Confirm each required specification individually: 110VAC charger, minimum
> 25-foot platform height, 500-pound platform capacity at maximum height,
> folding rails, zero turning radius, minimum 36-inch deck extension, dual
> flashing warning lights, and tilt alarm. Provide make/model, product
> literature, delivery lead time, payment terms, and written reseller or dealer
> authorization suitable for a federal RFQ.

## Sourcing clue

The [JLG ES2632](https://www.jlg.com/en/equipment/scissor-lifts/es-series/es2632)
is a useful starting point because JLG publicly lists a 25-foot 6-inch platform
height, 507-pound platform capacity, and zero-inch turning radius. It is not
confirmed compliant. Require the dealer to confirm the remaining features,
options, extended warranty, manuals, training, and authorized-reseller route.

Use JLG's [sales and service location finder](https://www.jlg.com/en/find-a-location)
to identify dealer contacts.

## Regulatory check

FAR [19.505](https://www.acquisition.gov/far/19.505?searchTerms=non+manufacturer+rule)
applies the nonmanufacturer rule to ordinary small-business set-asides above
the simplified acquisition threshold. FAR [2.101](https://www.acquisition.gov/far/2.101)
currently defines that threshold as `$350,000`. Do not assume the RFQ is below
that threshold until supplier pricing or NIST confirms it.
